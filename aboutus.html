Who are we?

We are a family owned and operated service company since 2005 that is constantly growing. With over years of experience, we have built a reliable client-base around the Denver Metro Area. We provide a variety of cleaning services in the residential, commercial and industrial market. The services include: Pressure Washing, Striping, Sweeping, Sign & Tire-Stopper installations & Removals, and many more. Regardless of the project being small or large, we will get it done. We believe it is our level of dedication and attention to detail that has kept our clients coming back. If it leaves a mark, we can get it done.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide reliable and awe-inspiring work to our customers. PECO's service will not be done until we LEAVE A MARK. Nothing is more valuable satisfying our cusomters with whatever service they request. We plan to expand our services all throughout Colorado and establish new contracts with new business's and cusotmers. We aspire to move our business outside of Colorado, into other states.
